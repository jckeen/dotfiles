### Key decisions made
- **Keep Opus 4.6 everywhere** — user prefers accuracy over cost savings; fewer corrections = faster overall
- **`--allowedTools` over `--dangerously-skip-permissions`** — scoped permissions by default, full auto is opt-in with `--full-auto` flag
- **Nothing ever pushes automatically** — `review-and-push.sh` is the only script with push access, and only after tests pass + AI review clears it
- **Agent Teams is a global toggle** — `CLAUDE_CODE_EXPERIMENTAL_AGENT_TEAMS` in settings/env, not per-agent config. ~15x token usage, use selectively
- **Deferred agents**: `cross-repo-tracker` (repos are distinct products), `incident-responder` (not in prod yet), `api-documenter` (low urgency)
